# TimesFlow as an ActivityWatch Alternative

Users looking for an ActivityWatch alternative are often comparing local-first tracking tools and want a product that feels easier to use every day.

## Why TimesFlow Is Relevant

TimesFlow is useful for people who want:

- automatic desktop tracking
- local-first behavior by default
- a cleaner user-facing productivity layer
- reports, goals, focus sessions, and easier review workflows

## Positioning Difference

Compared with ActivityWatch-style usage, TimesFlow places more emphasis on:

- a polished desktop product experience
- structured dashboard analytics
- long-term habit review through heatmaps and reports
- turning data into action with goals and focus tools

## Best-Fit Users

- professionals who want to review work patterns quickly
- founders and freelancers tracking real app usage
- students and knowledge workers reducing distraction
- users who want local-first tracking without a rougher workflow
