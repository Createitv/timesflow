# Privacy-Focused Time Tracker

Many users looking for a privacy-focused time tracker want automatic tracking without giving up full control of their data.

## Why TimesFlow Is Relevant

TimesFlow is positioned for users who care about:

- local-first storage
- offline-capable workflows
- optional rather than mandatory sync
- personal analytics without heavy cloud dependence

## What This Means in Practice

- tracking data is stored on the device by default
- the app remains useful even without constant connectivity
- users can export data when needed
- sync is an optional Pro workflow rather than the baseline requirement
